SENSATIONAL HYBRID SUPERCAR

One of a never-before kind of art. Our first V8, mid-engine, hybrid supercar. On its journey from concept to road-ready production reality. Bringing intensity to the driver’s seat as a truly driver focussed experience. Flooding perfection through every pore, part and particle. We’re excited for you to meet Valhalla.

Valhalla is the ultimate balance of elite performance, an evolution of the ground-breaking Aston Martin Valkyrie. Combining a full carbon fibre structure with sophisticated design materials. The result is an immersive new kind of supercar driving experience.

Strictly limited to just 999 examples, we are rewriting what best-in class means for driving dynamics and driving pleasure. Incorporating our learnings from Formula 1®, Valhalla is at the forefront of our transition from internal combustion to hybrid to full electrification. Confounding expectations, Valhalla is a plugin hybrid that commands over 1,000PS.
